Local Date Ideas

= Utah State University Activity

  1. Play magic, dnd, and other card / board games at Game Grid
  2. Make charcuterie board and watch sunrise/sunset at Hyrum Gibbon Mt. Logan Park
  3. Cooking class at Love To Cook
  4. Go to the D.I. and find each other an outfit for under $15 dollars
  5. Local Music at The Cache
  6. Jazz night (live music, food, dancing) at Elite Hall in Hyrum

  9. Weekend brunch at Herm's Inn if there is a wait, take a walk to first dam
  10. Get a drink from local coffee shops
    - Westside
    - Cafe Ibis
  11. Walk the Logan River Trail. You can make a loop with the Highland Trail. (~3.5 mi) Climb at Elevation
  12. Try something new and rent something from campus recreation. (ie. paddle boards, skis/snowboards, mountain bikes, camping gear, etc)
  13. Around holidays, take a picture with Santa at the mall
  14. Try great vegan food at Bloom
  15. Visit Tony Grove Lake. There is a small trial around the lake, paddle boarding, kayacking, or trail heads to Naomi Peak or White Pine (White pine is also a great beginner backpacking trail)
  16. Create a playlist together and take a scenic drive to Bear Lake. Enjoy local raspberry shakes. Raspberry Days (August 3rd-5th 2023)
  17. Ice Skating at George S. Eccles Ice Center
  18. Explore Logan's Art Scene: Visit the Nora Eccles Harrison Museum of Art at Utah State University or stroll through the Cache Valley Center for the Arts in downtown Logan to enjoy local art and performances
  19. Enjoy a night at the Utah Theatre: Catch a movie or live performance at the historic Utah Theatre in downtown Logan. The theatre has been beautifully restored and offers a unique atmosphere for a night out.
  20. Visit the American West Heritage Center: This living history museum showcases the history of the American West. You and your date can take a tour, watch a live demonstration, and learn about the pioneers who settled in the area.
  21. Enjoy a night at the Utah Theatre: Catch a movie or live performance at the historic Utah Theatre in downtown Logan. The theatre has been beautifully restored and offers a unique atmosphere for a night out.
  22. Hike to Wind Caves: Take a hike up to the Wind Caves in Logan Canyon. The trail is moderate in difficulty and offers breathtaking views of the surrounding mountains. Or take a less traveled hike across the canyon, Crimson trail. Crimson trail lines the top of spectacular cliffs and offers a different view of the wind caves.
  23. Visit Zootah and have a picnic at Willow Park after
  24. Visit Red Balloon Toys and find a project that you can do together
  25. Sled down the hill at Old Main on campus

  32. Take a polar bear plunge at second dam
  33. Go to the Logan LDS Temple: If you are a member of the LDS church, you can visit the Logan Temple and take a guided tour. The temple is a beautiful and peaceful place to spend some time.
