Rentals

There are tons of places in logan for rentals of all different sorts of things in winter and summer. We have so many beautiful bodys of water, ski sights and hiking trails. Sometimes it is fun to just walk around the lakes or feed the ducks, etc. But my favorite thing is to get out on the lake. Ain’t nobody got the money to buy 4 kayaks to try it out and even see if you like it! There are ton of places that do CHEAP rentals. Here are some of my faves.

Bad Apple Eddys first off they are the coolest people and offer TONs of discount on their rentals. They are really good at working with you and have Quality equipment. They only offer rentals on summer equipment and the quantity is limited.

USU Campus Rec another Great rental place is located on campus. Ther rent everything from kayaks, to tents, and are open for all seasons. If you are a student you get a 35% discount, but if not they still have compatible prices to other places in the valley.

Al's Sporting Goodsanother place that offers rentals is Als. While this is widely known, the rentals can be cheaper than some of the other places listed and they have many different things. I personally have rented a mountain bike here, skis for the skiing season, and shoe shoeing equipment! I’m not super educated at what else they have to offer
