Skiing/Snowboarding

Utah is known to have the greatest snow on Earth, so you get your moneys worth out of the city and enjoy the snow!

Cherry Peak This is an actual ski resort but they have lots of other fun stuff going on during all seasons. in the winter they have a tubing track that they groom out and they have a little escalator type thing that takes you back to the top for all of us lazy enthusiasts. its pretty cheap and comes with the tube rental, but its really fun. especially if you're not that into other snow sports. Another thing cherry peak does is concerts in the summer and you find all the info on their websites. Previous years they used to be at the resort, and that was a blast but I think they may have a concert hall that is near the fairgrounds now! the tickets were about 20 bucks and the concerts were all really fun.

Nordic United this is FREE. Nordic united grooms the snow for skiers in the winter at green canyon. . While it is not as nice as an actual ski resort, and only gets groomed a couple times a week, it is free to try out. You can snoe shoe to the top and ski down and it's a nice little trail.
